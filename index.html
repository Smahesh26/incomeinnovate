F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for a loan through Income Innovate?

Both individuals and businesses can apply. We offer customized loan solutions for entrepreneurs, SMEs, and students pursuing higher education in India or abroad.

Do you charge any fees for your services?

We maintain complete transparency. Any applicable processing fees or charges are clearly communicated upfront—no hidden costs.

What types of business loans do you offer?

We offer working capital loans, term loans, equipment financing, invoice financing, and expansion loans, among others.

What documents are required for a business loan?

Basic KYC documents, financial statements, bank statements, business registration proof, and ITRs are typically required. Our team will guide you through the specific needs.

Can I apply for an education loan if I haven’t received an admission letter yet?

Admission confirmation is usually required for disbursal, but pre-approval is possible in some cases. We’ll help you understand your options.

What expenses are covered under the education loan?

Our education loans can cover tuition fees, hostel or living expenses, books, travel (for international studies), exam fees, and more.
